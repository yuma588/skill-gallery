A Brief History

1990s - The Birth
Seo Taiji and Boys revolutionize Korean music with hip-hop influences, laying the foundation for modern K-pop.
2000s - Hallyu Wave
TVXQ, Girls' Generation, Super Junior lead the Korean Wave across Asia with polished idol groups.
2010s-Present - Global Domination
BTS, BLACKPINK, TWICE break international barriers, conquering Billboard, Grammys, and worldwide charts.
